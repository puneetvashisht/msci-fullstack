Exercise: Discounted Shopping Cart
Create a class ShoppingCart that allows adding items and applying discounts using lambda functions.

Requirements:
The ShoppingCart class should have:
    A method to add an item with its price.
    A method to calculate the total price of the cart.
    A method to apply a discount, which accepts a lambda function.
    
Use lambdas for:

Calculating a discount (e.g., 10% off, fixed amount discount, or buy-one-get-one-free).
Filtering items based on a condition (e.g., items above a certain price).
